Introduction (What is REdiC ?)

REdiC is a freely available database providing A-to-I editing events in 32 cancer types (~11,000 individuals) from TCGA project and functional prediction of these editing events. Unlike other resources, we identified hyper-edited reads (and sites) as well as gain/loss of miRNA-3'UTR interactions due to editing. In total, we collected (195,907 are novel) among wich XXXXX impact the binding of miRNA-3'UTR. REdiC provides a comprehensive resource for the functional prediction of RNA editing in human cancers.
